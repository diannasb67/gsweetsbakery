Chef Warren Sanchez

Passionate about his food, Chef Sanchez is unlike no other. His love for multicultural is unmeasurable. Born into an Italian and Puerto Rican family, food was part of his identity. He grew up in the Bronx and around Arthur Avenue and his high was from the sugar lingering in the air. He worked hard to replicate the taste he remembered as a kid. What “G Sweets” is all about is Family, Memories and “G” In remembrance of “G”warreno” my G”randfather, who was the “G”reatest Inspiration to me. Chef Sanchez is driven, he is creative and full of ambition something that you want and expect from a chef!
